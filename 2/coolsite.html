How-2-uze

Use that first form to add items to the todo list. Do what you gotta todo. Select which list to add that todo to. Then click "Add".

That second form is for finding those todo items. It will update automatically as you type stuff in. There's also a checkbox. Cool.

With todo items, you can:

  • Click the text to do the todo. The UTC time you did the do on that todo will also show up.
  • Click the text again to undo the do of that todo.
  • Click X to delete the todo. Don't worry, press ctrl+z, and it will be back in the same place you deleted it from.
  • Click on the list title to hide the list. Click on it again to show it.
  • Use that last form to add new todo lists.
  • That's it, you can stop reading now.
